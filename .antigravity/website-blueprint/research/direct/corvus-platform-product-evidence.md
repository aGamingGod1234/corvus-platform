## Product facts used by the design

- Corvus is a local-first AI workspace for governed, auditable work.
- One authoritative Python core serves the CLI, API, web client, and Windows desktop shell.
- Presentation adapts across Everyday/Developer and Individual/Team without creating new authority.
- Local execution is available on the current machine; Corvus Cloud remains Preview until its real lifecycle exists.
- Approvals, budgets, credentials, audit state, and kill switches remain server/domain authoritative.
- The shared product surface includes responsive web and desktop clients, durable workflows, conversations, resumable events, approvals, and recovery.
