# ChatGPT conversion reference evidence

- Source: https://chatgpt.com/
- Retrieved: 2026-07-16
- Retrieval: browser-readable public page after the direct HTTP research client encountered a Cloudflare challenge

## Observed interaction patterns

- A persistent `New chat` entry gives the primary action a stable home.
- Search and history stay in navigation rather than competing with the composer.
- The signed-out surface uses one calm, immediately understandable conversation entry point.
- Login is explicit and explains the continuity benefit without blocking comprehension of the product.
- The composer remains the center of gravity while secondary capabilities are progressively disclosed.

## Copy boundary

Use the hierarchy principles of a stable new-thread action, persistent history, and composer-led interaction. Do not copy ChatGPT wording, layout measurements, icons, navigation order, brand assets, or trade dress.
